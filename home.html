About me

I am a Junior (mainly) Front-End Developer with a strong Mathematics background

At the start of this year I began teaching myself the basics of computer programming and worked through a Java Fundamentals book completing all the exercises. Since then I have explored multiple areas of programming including front-end and back-end web development, creating mobile apps in Android, and even machine learning and data visualisation, all while being a full-time teacher.

I am eager to take the next step, expand my knowledge/experience and start using my skills for bigger projects than just my personal ones which are mainly just for fun or to practice. Check out the computer languages I have learnt so far and my portfolio to see if I would be a good fit for your team!
